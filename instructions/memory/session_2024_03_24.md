### 4. Binary effort model discovery (MAJOR)
- Identified that effort E ∈ {0.6, 0.8, 1.0} is perfectly confounded with distance D ∈ {1, 2, 3}
- With graded E, k·E absorbs all distance effects → λ=14 → S flat on distance
- Binary E (high=1, low=0) forces distance through S → λ=0.8 → S has real distance gradient
- **+88 ELBO improvement** — binary E is the better model
- E-scaling (E×D) tested and failed (ΔELBO = -293)

### 5. Effort-controlled vigor model
- excess = α + δ·(1−S) + γ·E_chosen with λ=0.8
- γ = -0.535 captures physical demand constraint
- ELBO +1870 over uncontrolled model

### 6. Allocation dimension analysis
- β-δ coupling defines threat-reactivity dimension (PC1=66%, equal loadings)
- **Angle predicts earnings (r=0.51) and escape (r=0.54); magnitude does not (p>0.1)**
- β-heavy people lose money (-61 pts, 18.5% escape); δ-heavy earn most (+51 pts, 51.1%)
- Capacity constraint visualized: threat boosts excess effort only when demand leaves headroom

### 7. Psychiatric analysis (Bambi multivariate)

- DASS Anxiety → β-heavy allocation (choice-stuck)
- "Adaptive apathy" interpretation: high-δ people report disengagement but mobilize under threat
